Question

What factors influence the frequency of interstate disputes in nations around the world?

Hypothesis

Natural resources, especially oil and petroleum, influence the frequency of interstate disputes in a nation. Developing and lower income nations with more oil and petroleum resources within their borders have higher frequencies of interstate disputes. Developed and high income nations with more oil and petroleum resources within their borders have lower frequencies of interstate disputes.
